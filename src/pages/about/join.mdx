## Volunteer Opportunities

SJIFR accepts applications for upcoming training throughout the year. Applicants are required to fill out an online application with District 3. Fill one out any time, and the District will contact you when a training is next available.

### Training class information

The 2026 training class is split into two sections:

* Structural Firefighting Academy: Jan 3rd through April 11th

* Wildland Firefighting Academy: April 25th through May 3rd

Training days will be generally Wednesday evenings and Saturdays.  A few Sundays will be included to handle specific skills and topics. [Academy FAQs](/assets/images/2026-academy-faq.pdf)

Feel free to contact our Training Officer at kkuetzing@sjifire.org with any questions.  Or come by Headquarters and meet the crews.
