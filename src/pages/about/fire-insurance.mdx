## District Fire Protection Overview

San Juan Island Fire Protection District #3 provides fire and rescue services to San Juan Island and surrounding islands. The district has a **Protection Class 6** rating from the Washington Surveying and Rating Bureau (WSRB). This rating applies to both the Town of Friday Harbor and the unincorporated district areas.

* **District Population (2024):**
  * Registered Voters: 7,200 (est.)
  * Permanent: 9,300 (est.)
  * Summer Effective Population: 21,000 (est.)

* **District Area:** 61 Sq. Miles - San Juan Island, including Town of Friday Harbor, as well as Brown, Henry, Johns, Pearl, Spieden, and Stuart Islands.

## Washington Survey & Ratings Bureau (WSRB)

### What is a Protection Class?

The WSRB protection class rating is used by insurance companies to help determine property insurance premiums. Class 1 represents the highest level of fire protection, while Class 10 indicates no recognized fire protection. A Class 6 rating reflects our district's combination of fire department capabilities, water supply infrastructure, and emergency communications.
