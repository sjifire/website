## Governance

* **Formal Name:** San Juan Fire Protection District #3

* **Organized:** 1958 under Title 52 Revised Code of Washington

* **Elected Board of Fire Commissioners:** Dwight Colley (Chair), Warren Appleton (Vice-Chair), Mike Coryell

* **Board Meetings:** The 2nd Tuesday each month @ 3:00PM.

see [Governance page](/about/governance) for details




## District

* **District Population (2024):**

  * Registered Voters: 7,200 (est.)
  * Permanent: 9,300 (est.)
  * Summer Effective Population: 21,000 (est.)
* **District Area:** 61 Sq. Miles - San Juan Island, including Town of
  Friday Harbor, as well as Brown, Henry, Johns, Pearl, Spieden, and Stuart
  Islands.
