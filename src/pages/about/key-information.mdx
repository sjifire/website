## Governance

* **Formal Name:** San Juan Fire Protection District #3

* **Organized:** 1958 under Title 52 Revised Code of Washington

* **Elected Board of Fire Commissioners:** Dwight Colley (Chair), Warren Appleton (Vice-Chair), Mike Coryell

* **Board Meetings:** The 2nd Tuesday each month @ 3:00PM.

see [Governance page](/about/governance) for details




## District

* **District Population (2024):**

  * Registered Voters: 7,200 (est.)
  * Permanent: 9,300 (est.)
  * Summer Effective Population: 21,000 (est.)
* **District Area:** 61 Sq. Miles - San Juan Island, including Town of
  Friday Harbor, as well as Brown, Henry, Johns, Pearl, Spieden, and Stuart
  Islands.

## Insurance Providers and Washington Survey & Ratings Bureau

San Juan Island Fire Protection District #3 has a **Protection Class 6** rating from the Washington Surveying and Rating Bureau (WSRB). This rating applies to both the Town of Friday Harbor and the unincorporated district areas.

* [About WSRB Protection Classes](https://www1.wsrb.com/resources/public-protection)
* [WSRB Protection Class FAQ](https://www1.wsrb.com/protection-class-faq)
* To verify your property's protection class: Call WSRB at [(206) 217-0101](tel:+12062170101) or email [customerservice@wsrb.com](mailto:customerservice@wsrb.com)

**Rating Reports:**
